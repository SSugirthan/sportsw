Football World League 2025

Date: March 20 - April 10, 2025
Location: National Stadium, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Get ready for the most anticipated football event of the year, featuring teams from 32 countries. Fans will witness high-intensity matches and unforgettable moments as the world's best players battle it out for glory. The opening ceremony promises a dazzling spectacle with performances by international artists.

Highlights of the event include exclusive fan zones, interactive games, and the chance to meet legendary football players. Don't miss this incredible experience that brings the world together through the beautiful game.

Cricket Champions Trophy 2025

Date: May 1 - May 31, 2025
Location: Melbourne Cricket Ground, Australia

The Cricket Champions Trophy returns with a bang as the top 10 cricketing nations compete in a month-long tournament. Expect electrifying matches filled with nail-biting finishes and extraordinary performances from cricket's biggest stars.

The venue will host various activities, including workshops for budding cricketers, memorabilia auctions, and live commentary by cricket legends. The final match is projected to attract millions of viewers worldwide.

Olympic Qualifiers for Swimming

Date: June 15 - June 30, 2025
Location: Aquatic Center, Tokyo, Japan

Witness the speed and precision of elite swimmers as they compete for a spot in the Olympics. The qualifiers feature events across multiple categories, including freestyle, butterfly, backstroke, and medley races.

Visitors can enjoy behind-the-scenes tours, athlete meet-and-greets, and live commentary. The event is a perfect opportunity to celebrate the spirit of competition and the dedication of world-class swimmers.

Wimbledon Tennis Championship

Date: July 1 - July 14, 2025
Location: All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club, London, UK

Join us for the most prestigious tennis tournament in the world. Wimbledon 2025 promises two weeks of thrilling matches, showcasing the skills of the greatest tennis players on the planet.

Enjoy traditional strawberries and cream while watching epic clashes unfold on the court. Don't miss the chance to witness history being made as players chase the coveted championship trophy.

World Athletics Meet

Date: August 10 - August 20, 2025
Location: Olympic Stadium, Berlin, Germany

The World Athletics Meet brings together the fastest, strongest, and most agile athletes from across the globe. Witness record-breaking performances in track and field events, including sprints, long-distance runs, pole vault, and high jump.

The event will also host cultural showcases, highlighting the heritage of participating countries. It's a celebration of human achievement and perseverance like no other.
